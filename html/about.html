Our Verification Process

Trust is our core value. Every property listing published on our platform goes through a multi-step verification sequence before it becomes searchable:

  • Phone Verification: Mandatory mobile number checks for all owners.
  • Document Screening: Spot checking ownership proofs and title records.
  • Geocoding Integrity: Automated location boundary checks and PACI coordinate matching.
