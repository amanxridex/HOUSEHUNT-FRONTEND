Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

At HouseHunt, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use our services, as well as some of the steps we take to safeguard information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you sign up via Google Auth, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Property Data: Details of properties you list, including images, location, pricing, and descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, search queries, and viewed listings.

2. How We Use Information

HouseHunt uses the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To facilitate communication between buyers, sellers, and agents.
  • To protect our users and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To personalize your experience and provide tailored property recommendations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of HouseHunt except in the following cases:

  • With your consent: When you choose to contact a property owner or agent, your basic contact details are shared to facilitate the transaction.
  • For legal reasons: We will share personal information if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation, or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to meet any applicable law or enforceable governmental request.

4. Data Security

We work hard to protect HouseHunt and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. We use secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase & Firebase) equipped with industry-standard encryption.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your Profile Settings. If you wish to completely remove your account and all associated data, you may contact our Support Team via the Help Center.
