Frequently Asked Questions

HouseHunt is a zero-brokerage real estate platform focused on Delhi NCR — Noida, Gurgaon, Delhi, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. Unlike 99acres or MagicBricks, HouseHunt connects buyers and renters directly with verified property owners, eliminating broker fees entirely. Listings are free to post and free to browse.

Yes, HouseHunt is completely free for both property owners and people looking to buy or rent. There are no brokerage fees, no listing fees, and no hidden charges. Owners can post their property for free and connect directly with interested buyers or tenants.

Open HouseHunt and select Noida from the city filter. Set property type to Apartment, select "For Rent", choose 2 BHK, and set your price range. All results will show direct owner listings in Noida within your budget — no broker involved.

Tap "Post Property" on the homepage, select whether you're an owner or agent, choose rent or sell, pick the property type (apartment, villa, plot, commercial), and fill in the details. Your listing goes live immediately and is visible to buyers across Delhi NCR.

HouseHunt verifies seller mobile numbers and connects buyers directly with owners, reducing the risk of fraudulent listings. We recommend buyers physically visit properties and verify documents before finalising any deal.

HouseHunt currently covers the full Delhi NCR region: Noida, Greater Noida, Gurgaon (Gurugram), Delhi, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad. We are expanding to more Indian cities including Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad.

HouseHunt lists residential and commercial properties: 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4+ BHK apartments; independent houses and villas; builder floors; investment plots and land; office spaces and commercial shops; and PG/co-living accommodations.

HuntAI is HouseHunt's upcoming AI-powered property search assistant. It will let users describe what they're looking for in natural language — for example, "show me 2 BHK flats in Sector 62 Noida under ₹25,000 with parking" — and instantly surface the best matches. HuntAI is currently in development.
